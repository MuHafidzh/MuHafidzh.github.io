About

I am an Electrical Engineering student at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS). I spend my time building embedded systems for robots writing firmware on STM32 and ESP32 microcontrollers, working with communication protocols like CAN bus, RS485, and UART, and designing control loops that make machines behave the way they should. I joined the RIVAL ITS robotics team in 2022 and have been competing in the Indonesian Robot Contest (KRI) since then. During my studies, I interned at CV Manufaktur Robot Industri where I worked on autonomous towing systems, pipe inspection robots, and agricultural robots, and at BRIN researching assistive robotics platforms.

Featured Project

Autonomous Vehicle — Cherry Omoda E5

Final Project · Electrical Engineering · ITS · 2025–Present

For my final project I contributed to converting a stock Cherry Omoda E5 into an autonomous research platform. I reverse-engineered the CAN bus to map steering, throttle, and braking signals, then designed an electronic actuator interface that lets a computer take over vehicle controls. The control layer uses asymmetric PID for longitudinal speed regulation and adaptive Pure Pursuit for path tracking, with redundant safety checks so the vehicle can always be brought back under manual control.
